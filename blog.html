About Me

I've been learning to code since May 2022. I decided to start keeping a blog about my journey into web development and programming in general.

While my immediate goal is to get a job as a front-end web developer, I want to keep pursuing programming and computer science to see where it will get me. I'm also interested in game development, artificical intelligence and machine learning, amd quantum computing.

Admittedly though, computers and programming, while fascinating, are not my major passions. I've always wanted to be a writer first and foremost, and I spent most of my free-time writing or reading canonical literary works so that I can hopefully one day push the forms of imaginative literature.
